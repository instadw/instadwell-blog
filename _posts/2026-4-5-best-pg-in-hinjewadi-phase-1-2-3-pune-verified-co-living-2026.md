## Biggest Mistake to Avoid: Phase Mismatch

The most avoidable mistake Hinjewadi renters make is paying for a Phase 1 PG while working in Phase 3. The straight-line distance between phases is under 4 km, but internal traffic means a daily commute of 60 to 90 minutes each way at peak hours. Over a month, that is 40 to 60 hours of commute that could be reduced to under 10 minutes by picking the right phase.

**Rule of thumb:** Pick a PG on the same side of the Wakad-Hinjewadi bridge as your office gate. If you work night shifts, Phase 3 properties inside gated townships are the safer and quieter option by a significant margin.

## AC PG in Hinjewadi: Is It Worth the Extra Cost?

Hinjewadi's elevation keeps peak summer temperatures 3 to 5 degrees cooler than central Pune, rarely crossing 36 degrees even in May. An AC PG in Hinjewadi is worth the extra spend if you work from your room during the day, are a light sleeper sensitive to humidity in June through September, or are already paying above Rs. 9,000 per month where AC is a reasonable expectation at that price point.

**The real cost:** AC electricity is almost never included in the base rent. Expect a separate sub-meter bill of Rs. 1,500 to Rs. 2,500 per month depending on usage. Always confirm the electricity billing method in writing before signing.

## Hidden Costs to Check Before Booking

**Hard Water:** Hinjewadi's water supply is known for high TDS levels, a documented cause of hair fall among long-term residents. Managed PGs rarely provide softeners. A WaterScience shower filter (Rs. 1,500 to Rs. 2,000 one-time) is the practical fix most long-term residents eventually buy.

**AC Billing:** Rarely bundled in base rent. Expect Rs. 1,500 to Rs. 2,500 extra per month on a sub-meter. Confirm this in writing before paying anything.

**Security Deposits:** The most common move-out dispute involves landlords retaining deposits citing painting or cleaning costs. Record a timestamped video walkthrough of the room on move-in day and share it on WhatsApp with the property manager as a date-stamped record.

**Food Quality Drift:** Several managed properties start strong on food quality and decline after the third month as occupancy stabilizes. Check Google reviews specifically for food-related complaints, not just the overall star rating.
