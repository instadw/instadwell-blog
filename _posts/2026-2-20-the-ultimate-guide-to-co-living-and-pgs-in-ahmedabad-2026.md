### Corporate PG in Ahmedabad

Corporate PG in Ahmedabad is concentrated along three corridors: Prahlad Nagar, SG Highway, and Bodakdev. These areas hold the highest density of IT parks, MNC offices, and BPO campuses in the city.

- Housekeeping at least 3x per week
- Power backup, essential for work-from-home setups
- High-speed Wi-Fi, 50 Mbps minimum
- AC rooms with per-unit billing, not a flat fee
