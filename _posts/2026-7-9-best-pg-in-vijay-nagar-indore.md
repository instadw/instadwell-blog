## Safety tips before you book

Never pay a full deposit before physically visiting the room or verifying it through a call with the resident manager. Ask for the exact floor, sharing type and whether AC and electricity are billed separately before you commit. If you are booking for a woman, confirm the warden's presence in person, not just in the listing description. Confirm the notice period and deposit refund policy in writing, verbally agreed terms are the most common source of disputes at move out.
