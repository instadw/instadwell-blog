## The Dark Side of Pune Rentals: 3 Scams to Watch Out For

Before you hand over cash, you need to know what you are up against. Community discussions and recent tenant reports highlight three major risks in the unorganized market.

### 1. The "Fake Agreement" & Deposit Trap

A common nightmare involves brokers providing "soft copy" agreements that have no legal standing.

Real World Case: A tenant recently reported being handed a simple Word document instead of a notarized rental agreement. When they tried to leave, the landlord refused to return the deposit, claiming there was no legal contract binding them to a refund.

The Fix: Always insist on a registered or notarized rent agreement before transferring the full security deposit.

### 2. The "Hidden Cost" Ambush (Financial & Lifestyle)

When a deal looks too good to be true, you are likely paying a hidden "cost" either in unexpected bills or a loss of basic comfort.

-   The Monetary Cost: Advertised rents often exclude essentials. A listing might claim ₹8,000/month, but after moving in, you are hit with separate, inflated bills for electricity, Wi-Fi, maintenance, and even "mandatory" meal plans that you didn't ask for.
    
-   The Lifestyle Cost (The Power Cut Reality): Distinct from money, you often "pay" with your productivity. In areas like Kharadi, power cuts can last from 1 AM to 5 AM. Unverified PGs often lack backup, meaning your fan, AC, and Wi-Fi die while you are trying to sleep or work. A "cheap" room becomes very expensive when you cannot get your work done.
    

### 3. The "No Bachelors" Last-Minute Shock

Unverified brokers often take a token amount, promising that a society allows bachelors. On moving day, the Society Management stops you at the gate.

Tenant Insight: One user shared that after paying brokerage, the society guard refused entry, stating a strict "Family Only" rule that the agent conveniently "forgot" to mention.

## General Questions

**Q: Are there luxurious PG options near colleges like SIT?**

Yes, luxury PGs and co-living spaces are available near major colleges. These typically offer premium amenities like gyms, reading rooms, and better food menus, but they come at a higher price point than standard hostels.

**Q: What are the best options for IT professionals in Hinjewadi?**

Many large apartment complexes (like those in Phase 1) function as high-quality PGs. These "flat-style" PGs are popular because they offer a community feel with the privacy of an apartment, often including internet and food in the rent.

**Q: Is it safe to rent in Kharadi?**

Kharadi is a popular IT hub with many budget-friendly options starting around ₹6,000. However, safety and power cuts are frequent concerns. Look for "tried and tested" properties that offer gated security and 24/7 power backup.

## Budget & Amenities

**Q: Can I find a private single room under ₹15,000?**

It is difficult but possible in older buildings or suburbs. In prime areas like Baner or Viman Nagar, private rooms usually start from ₹16,000 to ₹20,000. Under ₹15,000, you are more likely to find a premium double-occupancy room.

**Q: Should I opt for a PG with meals included?**

Tenant feedback is mixed. Some users complain that PG food quality declines over time. A common "pro-tip" is to choose a PG that allows you to opt-out of the meal plan so you can hire a tiffin service if the in-house food isn't to your taste.

**Q: How do I avoid losing my security deposit?**

The golden rule from seasoned Pune renters is: "Don't pay any deposit before visiting." Scammers often pressure you to pay online to "block" a room. Always verify the property in person and sign a legal agreement first.
