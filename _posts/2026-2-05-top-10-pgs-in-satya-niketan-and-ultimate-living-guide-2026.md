Not all lanes in Satya are created equal. The deeper you go, the cheaper (and more chaotic) it gets.

## Single Room PG in Satya Niketan

Single room PG in Satya Niketan comes in two very different tiers. Local, non-AC single rooms deep inside the lanes start around Rs. 6,000 to Rs. 7,000, unfurnished or minimally furnished, with shared washrooms and no standardized security.

A managed single room PG in Satya Niketan starts around Rs. 15,000, AC, attached washroom, and furnished as standard. At the premium end, fully furnished private studios run up to Rs. 40,000, closer to a serviced apartment than a traditional PG room.

If you're choosing between the two tiers, the deciding factor usually isn't price alone, it's whether you want a landlord relationship or a managed one. A single room in a landlord-run property means dealing directly with the owner for repairs, water, and electricity. A managed single room comes with app-based maintenance and standardized billing, which is worth the premium for most first-time South Campus students.
