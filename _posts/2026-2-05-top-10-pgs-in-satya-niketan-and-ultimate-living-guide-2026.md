## The "Lane" Logic: Where Should You Actually Live?

Not all lanes in Satya are created equal. The deeper you go, the cheaper (and more chaotic) it gets.

**The "Gurudwara" Zone (Lanes 1-5):**
    

**Verdict:** The Best Spot.
    
**Why:** It faces the park and the Gurudwara. It’s airy, accessible, and feels like a residential colony.
    
**Rent:** Slightly higher, but worth it for mental peace.
    

The "Deep Inside" Zone:
    

**Verdict:** Proceed with Caution.
    
**Why:** Residents describe it as "suffocating" and "congested." Ventilation is poor, and you might have smoke blown in your face the moment you step out.
    
**Rent:** Cheapest, but at the cost of hygiene.
    

Need more space? ##  The Commute: The "Venky" Walk

Brokers will tell you everything is "walking distance." In the Delhi summer (45°C), that is a lie.

If you are looking for a PG near Sri Venkateswara College, Satya Niketan is the default choice, but here is the commute reality:

-   To Venky / Motilal / ARSD:
    

**Walk:** Doable in winter (10-15 mins crossing the footover bridge).
    
**Auto:** In summer, take a shared auto from the Satya Market.
    
**The "Fair" Fare:** Standard rate is ₹10 - ₹20. Do not pay more.
    

 **The Metro Advantage:**
    

-   You are serviced by the Durgabai Deshmukh South Campus (Pink Line) station.1
    
-   **Hack:** If you live deep inside Satya, the walk to the metro can take 15 mins. ##  Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Is Satya Niketan safe for girls?**

Yes, primarily because it is student-heavy and active. However, internal lanes can be narrow and poorly lit at night. We recommend sticking to PGs near the main market road or opting for managed buildings like Stanza Living which have security guards and biometrics.

**What is the cheapest PG near Venky?**

You can find non-AC rooms deep inside Satya Niketan for as low as ₹6,000/month. For decent amenities (AC + Wifi), expect to pay around ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 in double sharing.

**Is Anand Niketan better than Satya Niketan?**

Anand Niketan is cleaner, quieter, and greener, but significantly more expensive (rents start at ₹18k+). Satya Niketan is congested but offers the true "campus vibe" and student-friendly food prices.
