## Hidden Charges Students Must Watch Out For

Hidden costs are one of the biggest problems students face. frequently reported in rental scam cases across [Indian cities](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/).

### Common Hidden Charges

-   “Visit” or booking fees
    
-   ID card or registration charges
    
-   Extra electricity charges (especially AC)
    
-   Internet or maintenance fees
    

### Important Rule

If someone asks for money just to visit a PG, it’s likely a scam.

Always ask for:

-   Complete rent breakup
    
-   Written list of inclusions
    
-   Clear deposit refund policy
    

----------

## How to Avoid PG Scams in Pune

Follow these safety rules:

-   Visit the PG in person
    
-   Talk to existing residents
    
-   Never pay before visiting
    
-   Don’t rely only on photos
    
-   Don’t share documents without verification
    

Many scams happen through WhatsApp groups and unverified brokers.
