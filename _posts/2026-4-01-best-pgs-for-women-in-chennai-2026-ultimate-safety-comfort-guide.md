### The Safety Corridor: Guindy vs. Pallavaram
If your workplace is near the airport or the southern business districts, you are likely choosing between these two hubs.

* **Guindy (The High-Visibility Choice):** Guindy is generally considered a safer and more developed hub. It is exceptionally well-lit, making the "last-mile" walk from the Metro station to your PG gate feel secure even after a late shift.
* **Pallavaram (The Proximity Choice):** While closer to the airport, some interior pockets in Pallavaram may not be as well-lit as the main GST Road. For Pallavaram and Urapakkam options in detail, see our [GST Road PG guide](/blog/best-pg-gst-road-pallavaram-urapakkam-chennai-2026).
