## North Campus PG Guide Hudson Lane vs. Vijay Nagar vs. GTB Nagar
If you have secured a seat in [SRCC](https://www.srcc.edu/), Hindu, [Hansraj](https://www.hansrajcollege.ac.in/), Kirori Mal, or [Miranda House](https://www.mirandahouse.ac.in/), congratulations. You have won the academic lottery.

Now comes the harder part: Finding a North Campus PG that doesn't bankrupt you or melt you in the Delhi heat.

North Campus isn't just one area; it’s a collection of distinct "micro-cities."

-   Hudson Lane: The "[South Delhi](https://instadwell.com/search?lat=28.4929198&lng=77.1847921&location=South%20Delhi%2C%20New%20Delhi%2C%20Delhi%2C%20India&city=New%20Delhi&country=India)" of North Campus (Cafes, Parties, Expensive).
    
-   Vijay Nagar: The "Budget Student" Hub (Narrow lanes, affordable, walkable).
    
-   GTB Nagar: The "Connector" (Metro access, busy, coaching centers).
    

This guide breaks down exactly where you should stay if you are looking for a PG near DU North Campus based on budget, noise, and college commute.

## The Rent Reality: Hudson Lane vs. Vijay Nagar

Let’s rip the band-aid off. Living on the "Cafe Strip" costs significantly more than a standard PG near North Campus.

| Feature                 | **Hudson Lane (The Premium Choice)**            | **Vijay Nagar (The Budget Hub)**                 |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Vibe**                | Posh, main road, “influencer” aesthetic         | Student-heavy, narrow lanes, cozy                |

**Insider Tip:** In Vijay Nagar, you will hear about "Single Storey" vs. "Double Storey" lanes.

-   **Single Storey:** Cheaper, but often older construction and feels more claustrophobic.
    
-   **Double Storey:** Slightly pricier but offers better ventilation and wider roads.

## FAQs

**Q: What is the rent for a PG in Vijay Nagar, Delhi?**
Vijay Nagar is the budget hub of North Campus. Non-AC shared rooms go for Rs. 6,000 to Rs. 8,000, while AC double-sharing rooms in managed buildings like Stanza Living range from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 14,000. Single rooms in the same managed properties start at Rs. 14,000+. For context, Hudson Lane commands a 30 to 40% premium over Vijay Nagar for equivalent room types.

**Q: Are there PGs in Vijay Nagar for girls?**
Yes. Vijay Nagar has several ladies-only and managed co-ed buildings. For female students, we specifically recommend managed operators like Stanza Living ([Penzance House](https://instadwell.com/properties/2753-penzance-house)) over independent landlord PGs - the internal lanes of Vijay Nagar can feel unsafe after 10 PM, and managed buildings offer biometric entry and security guards that local aunty PGs do not.

**Q: Is there a PG in Vijay Nagar under Rs. 10,000?**
Yes, budget options in Vijay Nagar start from Rs. 6,000 for non-AC shared rooms in older constructions. However, below Rs. 8,000 you are typically looking at no power backup, shared washrooms, and limited ventilation on lower floors. If your budget is tight, Vijay Nagar is your best bet in North Campus - but visit in person before paying any deposit.

**Q: Is there a single room PG in Vijay Nagar, Delhi?**
Single rooms in Vijay Nagar are available but limited. Most budget buildings offer double or triple sharing. For a guaranteed single private room, look at managed co-living buildings in GTB Nagar which offer single-occupancy options starting around Rs. 14,000 to Rs. 16,000 with AC and attached washroom.

**Q: What are the PG fees in GTB Nagar, Delhi?**
GTB Nagar sits between Vijay Nagar (cheapest) and Hudson Lane (premium) on the rent spectrum. Managed PGs here start at Rs. 13,500 for shared rooms and go up to Rs. 19,000 for premium single rooms with AC, Wi-Fi, and meals. Budget options in the Nehru Vihar pocket near the drain go as low as Rs. 5,000 but without managed amenities.

**Q: Are there PGs in GTB Nagar for girls under Rs. 5,000?**
Under Rs. 5,000 in GTB Nagar means local unmanaged rooms in Nehru Vihar or narrow lanes near the Nullah. These are available but come with significant trade-offs - no security infrastructure, shared common areas, and variable hygiene. For female students, we strongly recommend spending slightly more (Rs. 8,000 to Rs. 10,000) for a managed building with biometric access, as personal safety in this price band is a real concern.

**Q: What is the difference between PG hostel fees and PG rent in GTB Nagar?**
"Hostel fees" in GTB Nagar usually refers to dormitory-style student hostels attached to coaching institutes, which charge Rs. 4,000 to Rs. 6,000 on a per-bed basis with very basic amenities. Standard PG rent (Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 19,000) includes a proper room, meals, Wi-Fi, and power backup. If you are here for UPSC or competitive exam prep and are on a strict budget, a hostel works for the short term - but for a stay beyond 3 months, a managed PG is worth the premium for quality of sleep and study.

**Q: What are the PG fees near North Campus, Delhi University?**
PG fees near North Campus vary heavily by zone. Vijay Nagar: Rs. 6,000 to Rs. 14,000. Hudson Lane: Rs. 14,000 to Rs. 22,000. GTB Nagar: Rs. 8,000 to Rs. 19,000. These ranges cover shared to private rooms across both local and managed operators. The biggest factor is whether electricity is sub-metered - always ask before signing, as an extra Rs. 2,500 to Rs. 3,500 per month in electricity can make a "cheap" PG expensive in practice.

**Q: What is the best PG hostel near North Campus for girls?**
For female students, the safest and most reliable options near North Campus are managed buildings on Hudson Lane ([Stanza Living San Diego House](https://instadwell.com/properties/2759-san-diego-house), [Penzance House](https://instadwell.com/properties/2753-penzance-house)) and in GTB Nagar ([Stanza Living Bukhara House](https://instadwell.com/properties/2769-bukhara-house), [Nuremberg House](https://instadwell.com/properties/2768-nuremberg-house)). These offer biometric entry, 24/7 security, and standardized amenities. Avoid single-owner buildings deep inside the Vijay Nagar lanes unless you have a trusted reference - the internal lanes are poorly lit and difficult to navigate alone at night.

**Q: Is there a single room PG near North Campus, Delhi?**
Yes. Single private rooms near North Campus are available primarily through managed operators rather than local landlords. Expect to pay Rs. 14,000 to Rs. 20,000 for a single AC room with attached washroom. Buildings on Hudson Lane and the main GTB Nagar road offer the most options. If a single room in Vijay Nagar is advertised under Rs. 10,000, verify the room size and ventilation in person - "single room" often means a converted storage space in older constructions.
