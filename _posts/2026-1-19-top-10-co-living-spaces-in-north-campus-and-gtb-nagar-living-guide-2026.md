Always confirm power backup and AC before signing, it is not a luxury here, it is an academic necessity.

**Insider tip:** in Vijay Nagar, you will hear about single storey versus double storey lanes.

- Single storey: cheaper, but often older construction and feels more claustrophobic.
- Double storey: slightly pricier but offers better ventilation and wider roads.

## PG Near Delhi University North Campus, What Changes By College {#pg-near-delhi-university-north-campus}

PG near Delhi University North Campus searches usually come from students who already know their college but not yet the area, so proximity to the specific campus matters more than the zone name.

- SRCC and Hindu College: Vijay Nagar is the closest walkable zone, most students reach either college in under 15 minutes on foot.
- Hansraj and Kirori Mal: also within Vijay Nagar walking distance, with GTB Nagar as the metro linked alternative.
- Miranda House: a short rickshaw ride from Vijay Nagar, or a 10 minute e-rickshaw from GTB Nagar.
- Ramjas and St Stephen's: closer to the Kamla Nagar side, Hudson Lane is the nearest of the three main zones covered here.

If your college is not listed above, the general rule holds. Vijay Nagar wins on walking distance and budget, GTB Nagar wins on metro access, Hudson Lane wins on building quality and lifestyle.
