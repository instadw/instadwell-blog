## Anna Nagar vs. Nungambakkam: Know the Difference

Anna Nagar is a large, planned residential neighbourhood built on a grid system, with numbered avenues (1st through 13th) and wide, tree-lined streets. It is famous for its organized blocks, Tower Park, and the presence of Shankar IAS Academy on 4th Avenue, which draws UPSC aspirants from across India every year.

Nungambakkam sits about 5 km south of Anna Nagar proper. It is more commercial and compact, with quick access to Anna Salai, Sterling Road, and the central business district. By metro, the two neighbourhoods are about 4 minutes apart via the Green Line.

Most managed PGs you will find in the ₹15,000 to ₹20,000 range are actually in Nungambakkam, not Anna Nagar proper. This is not a downside. Nungambakkam gives you faster access to offices, hospitals, and the metro. Anna Nagar gives you quieter blocks, parks, and a more residential feel.
