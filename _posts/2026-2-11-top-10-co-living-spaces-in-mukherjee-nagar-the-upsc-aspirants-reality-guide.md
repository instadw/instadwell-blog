## The Rent Map: Batra vs. "Crossing the Drain"

Mukherjee Nagar has a very specific geography of cost.

### 1. The "Batra Cinema" Premium (Mukherjee Nagar Proper)

**Location:** The heart of the chaos. Right next to the coaching centers and Aggarwal Sweets.
    
**Reality:** Landlords here still demand sky-high rents because of the "Brand Value."
    
**Rent:** A matchbox-sized single room costs ₹15,000 - ₹18,000.
    
 **Verdict:** Not worth it unless you have FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out).
    

### 2. The "Budget" Shift: Nehru Vihar & Gandhi Vihar

**Location:** Just "cross the drain" (Nullah).
    
**The Arbitrage:** Walking 10 minutes saves you 40% on rent.
    
**Rent:** Single rooms go for ₹8,000 - ₹10,000.
    
**Verdict:** Good for budget, but hygiene and ventilation are major issues here.
    

### 3. The "Smart" Zone: GTB Nagar & Model Town

**Location:** 5-10 minutes by e-rickshaw.
    
**Why it wins:** You escape the congestion and get clean, managed buildings (Stanza/Zolo) with Power Backup (essential for summer study sessions) and wider, open roads.
    
**Rent:** Comparable to Batra (₹12k-16k) but you get amenities instead of just a location tag.

Since many classes have moved online or to hybrid models, your daily life doesn't revolve around the classroom anymore, it revolves around the Library.

**Cost:** Library seats cost ₹1,500 - ₹2,000/month.
    
**Strategy:** Don't pay premium rent just to be near a coaching center you visit twice a week. Live where you sleep better.
