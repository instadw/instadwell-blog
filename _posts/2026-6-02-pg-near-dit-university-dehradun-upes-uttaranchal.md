## PG in Bidholi Dehradun {#pg-in-bidholi-dehradun}

Bidholi is home to the UPES Energy Acres campus and is one of the most searched student accommodation areas in Dehradun. A handful of local PGs operate directly in Bidholi, but the managed co-living inventory there is thin.

The practical reality for most students is that living in Bidholi itself means limited food delivery access, no Blinkit or Swiggy coverage, fewer grocery options, and restricted city transport on weekends. Many UPES students who initially try PG in Bidholi Dehradun end up relocating to Pondha after one semester because of these daily friction points.

If you are comparing PG in Bidholi against a Pondha option with meals included, factor in what you spend daily on food and transport before deciding. The commute from Pondha to UPES Bidholi campus is typically 10 to 15 minutes by shared auto or cab.
