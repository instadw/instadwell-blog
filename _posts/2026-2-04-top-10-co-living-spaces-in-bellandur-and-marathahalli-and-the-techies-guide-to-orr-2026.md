## Bellandur vs. Marathahalli: The Price War

While they are neighbours, the rental markets are distinct.

-   Marathahalli: The budget king. Older buildings, bustling streets, but incredibly cheap food and rent. Perfect for students and freshers.
    

-   InstaDwell Starting Rent:  ₹9,000/month (Sharing)
    

-   Bellandur: The premium choice. Newer buildings, cleaner layouts (like Green Glen), and closer to the biggest tech parks.
    

-   InstaDwell Starting Rent:  ₹11,000/month (Sharing)
    

The Verdict: If you want to save ₹2,000/month, choose Marathahalli. If you want a slightly quieter life, choose Bellandur.

## Where to Live: Commute & Convenience

Don't just search "Bellandur." You need to know which specific layout works for your office location.

### 1. Green Glen Layout (Bellandur)

-   The Commute: ~15 mins by vehicle to RMZ EcoWorld / EcoSpace.
    
-   The Vibe: Peaceful, residential, and full of parks. It’s the "HSR of Bellandur."
    
-   Best For: Professionals who want a calm environment after the daily grind.
    

### 2. Munnekollal (Marathahalli)

-   The Commute: ~11 mins walk (or 3 mins by vehicle) to Prestige Tech Park.
    
-   The Vibe: Crowded, bustling with "Andhra Messes," and very affordable.
    
-   Best For: Freshers working at Prestige Tech Park or nearby offices who want to minimize travel time.

## The "Water" Question (Crucial)

Bellandur is notorious for water shortages in summer.

-   The Reality: Groundwater is depleting, and many buildings rely on private tankers.
    
-   What to Check: Don't just trust the "24/7 Water" tag online. When we redirect you to the operator to complete your booking, ensure you ask the property manager specifically: "What is your tanker schedule in May?" Reliable operators will have a clear contingency plan, while others might not.

## The Ultimate "Move-In" Checklist: What to Ask

Before you pay that token advance, run through this 5-point check during your visit. If you get the "Ideal Answer", it's a safe bet.

1.  Signal Check: Walk into the specific room (and the washroom) to check your mobile data speed. Bellandur basements are notorious for dead zones.
    

    ✅ Ideal Result: 4G/5G bars are stable inside the room, or the property has commercial-grade Wi-Fi routers on every floor.
    

2.  The "May" Question: Ask the warden/manager: "What happens to the water supply in May?"
    

    ✅ Ideal Answer: "We have a priority contract with private tanker vendors and large underground storage. We haven't faced a total dry-out in the last year."
    

3.  Hidden Costs: Clarify how electricity is charged.
    
    ✅ Ideal Answer: "It is included in the rent" OR "You have a separate sub-meter, and we charge standard rates (approx. ₹8-10/unit), not arbitrary fixed fees."
    

4.  Lock-in Logic: Confirm the lock-in period and notice period.
    

    ✅ Ideal Answer: "There is a short lock-in (1-3 months), and you just need to give 30 days' notice before leaving to get your full deposit back."
    

5.  Traffic Test: Visit the property at 9:00 AM on a weekday.
    
    ✅ Ideal Result: You can hear the traffic hum but not individual horns from your bed, and you can reach the main ORR junction in under 10 minutes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a metro station in Bellandur?** 

Not yet. The ORR Metro line is under construction (causing the traffic!). Until it opens, living close to your office in layouts like Munnekollal or Green Glen is your best "Metro alternative."

**Q: Which area is safer for women?** 

 Green Glen Layout (Bellandur) is generally considered safer and more family-oriented with well-lit streets compared to the denser lanes of Munnekollal.

**Q: Can I skip the traffic on a bike?** 

Yes, many locals use the "Yemalur Shortcut" to bypass the main ORR traffic when heading towards Old Airport Road, though it can get tricky during rains.
