While the Coimbatore Metro remains in the planning stages, local infrastructure has improved. The new foot overbridge near PSG College has made crossing Avinashi Road safe for pedestrians.
* **PSG Tech / CIT:** 5 to 10 mins via bike or foot. City bus route 19A and route 5 both stop at the PSG College gate on Avinashi Road, running every 12 to 15 minutes during college hours. 
* **Tidel Park Coimbatore:** 10 to 12 mins via Avinashi Road. Shared autos from Peelamedu junction toward Race Course Road pass the Tidel Park stretch and are available from 7 AM. Fare is approximately Rs. 15 to Rs. 20 per person.
* **Airport:** 10 to 15 mins by road. No direct bus from Peelamedu to the terminal, autos and app cabs are the standard option. Pre-booked cabs run Rs. 120 to Rs. 150 for the terminal drop.
* **SKCET / Kuniyamuthur:** Shared autos from Peelamedu junction via Avinashi Road southbound cover the route in 15 to 20 minutes during off-peak hours.

Being near the airport and main road brings noise challenges. Modern co-living buildings in Peelamedu use better soundproofing (like double-glazed windows) than traditional hostels, but we still recommend properties at least 300m inside the main road for the best study environment.

### 3. Who should NOT choose Peelamedu?
If you prefer quieter residential areas or lower rents, Saravanampatti or outskirts like Kalapatti may be better suited. Peelamedu is ideal for proximity, not isolation. The high density of students and transit activity means it is rarely "quiet" compared to the developing suburbs.
