## Before You Pay a Deposit: South Delhi PG Scam Checklist
Broker scams are endemic in Saidulajab, Khirki, and Kishangarh. Here is what separates a safe move from a nightmare one.

*   **Get everything in writing.** Verbal agreements on rent and inclusions are routinely revised upward after you move in. Insist on rent, deposit amount, lock-in period, and all inclusions in a signed document.
*   **The ₹500 stamp paper trap.** Any rental agreement made on a sub-₹500 stamp paper has no legal standing in Indian courts and will not protect your deposit. Insist on a properly registered agreement.
*   **Reverse-search listing photos.** Fake "Saket" and "Malviya Nagar" listings reuse the same room photos across multiple cities. Run the listing image through Google reverse image search before visiting.
*   **Never pay before visiting.** Any booking or processing fee demanded before a physical visit is a red flag. Walk away.
*   **Deposit refund policy in writing.** Independent landlords in Khirki and Saidulajab routinely cite fabricated property damage to withhold deposits. Managed operators (Zolo, HelloWorld) have standardized, documented refund processes.
*   **Power and water check.** Ask current residents, not the broker, about summer power cuts and tap water quality. In unmanaged buildings, these are the two most common post-move-in complaints in this belt.
