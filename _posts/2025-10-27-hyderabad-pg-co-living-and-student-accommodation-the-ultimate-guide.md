## **Types of Accommodations Available**

### Co-Living Spaces

Modern, fully managed homes designed for millennials and Gen Z. These offer **furnished rooms, Wi-Fi, meals, housekeeping, and community activities,** perfect for professionals and students wanting comfort and networking opportunities.

### PG / Paying Guest Accommodations

The traditional go-to choice for budget-conscious tenants. PGs offer **shared or private rooms**, usually with meals, laundry, and internet. They’re simple, practical, and ideal for students or freshers.

### Student Hostels Near Universities

Located near campuses like **Osmania University**, **UoH**, and **JNTU**, these are tailored to student schedules and budgets. They often include mess facilities, study spaces, and flexible rent terms.

## **Why PGs, Co-Living, and Student Housing Are Booming in Hyderabad**

Several trends are driving this surge:

* **Proximity:** Many PGs are within walking distance of tech parks or universities.

## **How to Choose the Ideal Area**

When shortlisting, prioritize:

* **Commute:** Walkable or <30 min to your college/office.
* **Connectivity:** Metro, buses, and ride-share availability.
* **Social life:** Cafes, gyms, recreation nearby.
* **Safety:** Check lighting, patrol, and PG security.
* **Medical access:** Nearby hospitals or clinics.

## **FAQs**

**Q1. What’s the average cost of a PG in Hyderabad?**
Between ₹5,000 and ₹12,000, depending on area and amenities.

**Q2. Which is the safest area for women’s PGs?**
Hitec City, Gachibowli, and Kondapur, most have biometric entry and CCTV.

**Q3. Are PGs near IT hubs expensive?**
Slightly, but they save you commute time and transport costs.

**Q4. Can I find single-occupancy rooms?**
Yes, especially in premium PGs or co-living setups.

**Q5. Is food included in PG rent?**
Usually yes — breakfast and dinner; lunch optional or extra.

**Q6. What’s better: PG or Co-Living?**
PGs = cheaper.
Co-Living = better amenities, flexibility, and community.

**Q7. How do I book without brokerage?**
Use verified portals like InstaDwell or official brand sites.

**Q8. Are PGs near universities affordable?**
Yes, especially around Kukatpally (JNTU) and Tarnaka (Osmania U).

**Q9. What documents are needed?**
Aadhaar, college/office ID, and basic rental agreement.

**Q10. Can I switch PGs mid-contract?**
Depends on lock-in clause; most allow with 30-day notice.
