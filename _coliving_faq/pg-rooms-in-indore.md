PG rooms in Indore span a wide price range depending on locality, sharing type and furnishing quality. At the budget end, HelloWorld Fairy and HelloWorld Finch in Vijay Nagar both start at Rs. 5000 per month for a shared room with basic furnishing and RO water. In the mid range, Lucerne House at Rs. 7999 and Pretoria House at Rs. 9599 add air conditioning and meals. At the premium end, HelloWorld Luxe in Vijay Nagar tops the current verified list at Rs. 20000 per month for a fully furnished single room. Rent generally rises with proximity to the main Vijay Nagar square and falls as you move toward Bhawarkua or Rau.
