In 2026, the city-wide average for a co-living bed in Bengaluru sits near **Rs 12,900 per month**, though your actual quote depends on locality and operator. Budget brands like Zolo often start between **Rs 7,000 and Rs 9,000** in corridors such as Electronic City or Marathahalli, while Stanza and HelloWorld cluster closer to **Rs 12,000–18,000** in Koramangala, HSR, and Bellandur.

Furnishing, Wi-Fi, and housekeeping are typically bundled, so compare against **furnished 1 BHK averages (~Rs 19,000+)** rather than unfurnished flat headline rents. Always confirm whether food, power backup, and deposits are included before you sign.
